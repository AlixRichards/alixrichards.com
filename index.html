About Me

I am a front-end developer at creative advertising agency Dare West based in Bristol. I work mainly with HTML5, CSS3/SASS and Javascript. Beginning my career as a design graduate I soon realised I had more fun making things work than designing them. I like solving problems. I enjoy all kinds of projects from email marketing campaigns to HTML5 iPad ads to fully fledged websites.

Away from work I like playing with my Raspberry Pi and have a soft spot for running, crime dramas, diet coke and lots of very shiny sparkly things.
